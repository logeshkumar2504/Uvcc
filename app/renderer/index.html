About Camera Lister

What is Camera Lister?

Camera Lister is a desktop application that helps you discover and list all UVC (USB Video Class) compatible cameras connected to your system.

Supported Cameras

  • Logitech C920/C922 HD Pro Webcam
  • Elgato Facecam
  • Microsoft LifeCam Studio
  • Any UVC-compatible camera
